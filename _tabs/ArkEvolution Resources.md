## Benchmark download

> The ArkEvolution benchmark contains real-world ArkTS repository upgrade tasks.
> Each task is defined as a 7-tuple:
> `Ui+j = <Vi, Vi+j, Vi', Vi+j', Delta1, Delta2, M>`.
> `Vi` and `Vi+j` are the ArkTS versions before and after evolution;
> `Vi'` and `Vi+j'` are the corresponding JS/TS versions before and after evolution;
> `Delta1` records ArkTS syntax-adaptation changes;
> `Delta2` records upstream JS/TS evolutionary changes;
> `M` records the mapped entities that need synchronized evolution.
